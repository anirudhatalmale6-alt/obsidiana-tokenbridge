Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This site is operated by Obsidiana TokenBridge, a DBA under Resilience Ventures Inc. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit the Site, submit inquiries, or otherwise interact with us.

By using the Site, you consent to the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Site.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to respond to your inquiries and operate the Site:

  • Personal Information You Provide: When you fill out contact forms, book a call, or email us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, job title, and any message or details you voluntarily share.
  • Automatically Collected Information: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and session data (via server logs or basic analytics tools).
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use essential cookies for site functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies for targeted advertising.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (e.g., health, biometric, racial, or financial account information) or data from children under 18.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your inquiries and providing consulting information about real-world asset (RWA) tokenization services.
  • Scheduling calls or follow-ups.
  • Improving the Site's functionality and user experience.
  • Complying with legal obligations and protecting our rights.

We limit collection to what is adequate, relevant, and reasonably necessary, in line with the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA) and Delaware Personal Data Privacy Act (DPDPA).

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information, use it for targeted advertising, or share it for profiling that produces legal or significant effects.

We may share information only in limited cases:

  • With service providers (e.g., website hosting, email tools, or scheduling platforms) who are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • With professional advisors (e.g., lawyers or accountants) when necessary for service delivery or compliance.
  • If required by law, subpoena, or government request.
  • In the event of a business transfer (merger, acquisition), with appropriate protections.

All sharing complies with TDPSA, DPDPA, and Delaware Online Privacy and Protection Act (DOPPA) requirements.

4. Your Privacy Rights

As a resident of Texas or Delaware (or where applicable under U.S. law), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know/Access: Confirm whether we process your data and request a copy.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of any future processing for purposes not originally disclosed (we do not engage in sales or targeted advertising, so this is not applicable in most cases).
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

To exercise any right, email us with a clear description of your request. We will respond within 45 days (or as required by law) and provide an appeal process if needed. You may also use universal opt-out preference signals where supported (effective under DPDPA by January 1, 2026).

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above or as required by law (typically 1–3 years after your last interaction, unless longer retention is legally mandated).

7. Children's Privacy

The Site is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately.

8. International Users

If you access the Site from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. We take steps to ensure appropriate protections.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy at any time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates changes. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. We will notify you of material changes via the Site or email when feasible.
